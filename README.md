# Ontomeel.com - Your Ultimate Digital Bookshop & Library

Welcome to **Ontomeel.com**, the ultimate destination for book lovers! We have designed a seamless, intuitive, and modern platform that caters to all your reading needs—whether you prefer to buy books to keep forever, pre-order upcoming bestsellers, or borrow from our extensive library through our premium membership plans.

## 🌟 The Customer Journey & Workflows

### 1. Seamless Onboarding & Secure Accounts
* **Account Creation**: Customers can easily sign up for an account to start their reading journey.
* **Verified Access**: To ensure a secure community, every customer must verify their email address via a one-time password (OTP) before they can fully access their personalized dashboard.
* **Profile Management**: From the dashboard, users can update their profile information, manage multiple delivery addresses, and securely change their passwords.

### 2. Browsing & Discovering Books
* **The Bookstore**: Browse a massive collection of books categorized by genre, author, and new arrivals.
* **Detailed Book Profiles**: Every book has a dedicated page showcasing its cover, author, subtitle, page count, language, condition, edition, and real-time stock availability.
* **The Digital Library**: A separate, curated section strictly for members who wish to borrow books rather than purchase them directly. 

### 3. Flexible Purchasing Options
* **Direct Purchases**: Add your favorite books to the cart and proceed to standard checkout. We support seamless, instant payments via the **bKash** payment gateway.
* **Pre-Ordering**: Excited about an upcoming release? Reserve your copy early through our dedicated pre-order system, guaranteeing you get the book as soon as it launches.
* **Wallet System (Add Fund)**: Customers can top up their digital wallet balance in advance. This stored value makes future purchases and membership renewals frictionless and instantaneous.

### 4. The Library Membership Experience
Why buy when you can borrow? Ontomeel.com acts as a digital library for avid readers.
* **Tiered Subscriptions**: Customers can subscribe to one of our membership plans. 
* **Borrowing Privileges**: Depending on the chosen membership tier, users can borrow physical books for a set period. Books are delivered to their specified address and collected when the borrowing period is over.

### 5. Managing Your Reading Life
* **Comprehensive Dashboard**: Track the status of every order, pre-order, and borrowed book in one place.
* **Order Modifications**: Customers have the flexibility to cancel pending orders or borrowing requests if their plans change.
* **Reading Progress Tracker**: Readers can log into their dashboard to update and track how far along they are in their current reads, encouraging a consistent reading habit.

## 🛠️ The Administrator Workflow (Platform Management)

Behind the scenes, Ontomeel.com equips platform administrators with powerful tools to ensure a smooth operation:

* **Inventory Management**: Admins can easily add new books to the catalog—uploading covers, setting descriptions, updating stock counts, and flagging items as available for purchase, borrowing, or pre-order.
* **Order Fulfillment**: From the moment a customer places an order until it arrives at their door, admins track and update the order status (e.g., Processing, Shipped, Delivered) in real-time.
* **Pre-Order Tracking**: Special dashboards allow admins to manage high-volume pre-orders and clean up completed requests.
* **Manage Returns**: A dedicated system to handle books returned by library members, automatically updating inventory and making the books available for the next reader.
* **Payment Reconciliation**: Manage and verify bKash payments, and manually adjust wallet balances or transaction statuses if customer support is required.
* **Automated Communications**: The system automatically emails customers with order confirmations, OTPs for security, status updates, and renewal reminders so the admins can focus on growing the business.
